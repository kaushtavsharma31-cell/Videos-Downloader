About Us

Welcome to Downly, a dedicated downloader that helps users save Pinterest videos, idea pins, GIFs, and images quickly, easily, and securely. We offer a 100% free web utility focused on extracting multimedia from Pinterest in high definition (HD).

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the simplest and most secure Pinterest downloading experience online. We prioritize user privacy. That is why Downly is completely free to use, runs directly in your web browser with no software installation, and requires no registration or personal accounts.

Key Features

  • Download Pinterest Videos – Fetch MP4 videos from standard pins easily.
  • Download Idea Pins – Save modern Pinterest story/idea format layouts.
  • Download Pinterest GIFs – Save animated GIF formats directly to your device.
  • Download Pinterest Images – Extract high-resolution static images.
  • HD Quality Downloads – Preserve the original resolution of videos and photos.
  • Fast Processing & Free Forever – Instant server response with absolutely zero caps.

How We Do It

Our server-side scraping engine fetches content directly from public CDNs, parses the page to locate the original source file link, and allows you to download it directly. Everything happens securely without leaving your web browser.
