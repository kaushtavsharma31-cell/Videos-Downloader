Privacy Policy

At Downly, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information collected and how we handle it.

Pinterest URL Processing

Downly processes Pinterest video and pin URLs that you submit to extract the direct multimedia file link. This processing is performed entirely anonymously and temporarily on our server. We do not store or keep copies of the media you download, nor do we save your download history, submitted URLs, or details of your Pinterest account.

Anonymous Downloading

We do not collect, save, or store any personal identification info, social media login accounts, credentials, tokens, or security details. All scraping actions are performed completely anonymously. Download requests are handled server-side dynamically and files are passed straight to your browser stream.

Log Files

Downly follows a standard procedure of using log files. These logs register visitors when they access websites. The details include Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamps, and referring/exit pages. This information is used solely to evaluate website analytics, administer the site, and secure the hosting server.

Cookies & Web Beacons

Like any other website, Downly may use cookies to store information including visitors' preferences and browser configurations. This is used to optimize the user experience by customizing web page content based on visitors' browser types and settings.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

Downly's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or external websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers or networks for more detailed information.
